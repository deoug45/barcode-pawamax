How Barcode Scanning Works

  1. The EAN-13 barcode contains a unique 13-digit number
  2. When scanned, most modern barcode scanners can be configured to open a URL
  3. The URL is constructed as: https://yourdomain.com/product.html?code=[barcode-number]
  4. The product.html page reads the barcode number and displays the product information
  5. Note: Standard barcode scanners typically just input the number. For automatic URL redirection, you'll need:
    • A smartphone with a QR/barcode scanner app that supports URL schemes, OR
    • A programmable barcode scanner configured to append your domain URL, OR
    • Use the test button above to manually open the product page
